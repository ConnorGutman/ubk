Urban
Beekeeping?

Bees are crucial to agricultural production, but in recent years have been undergoing colony collapse disorder (CCD) due to habitat loss, climate change, and an array of other issues. Many cities are discovering the benefits of beekeeping towards both combatting declining bee populations, and in helping boost local agriculture and business efforts. The result is the field of Urban Beekeeping.
